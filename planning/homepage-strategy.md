# Oceana Yacht Charter — Homepage Conversion Strategy

---

## 1. Positioning

### Target Customer

**Primary**
- Affluent couples and families (2–6 people)
- Seeking a once-in-a-lifetime Alaska experience
- Prior travel: cruise ships, luxury resorts
- Value: privacy, exclusivity, curated experience
- Budget: ~$70,000+ acceptable if experience justifies it

**Secondary**
- Corporate groups
- Milestone trips (anniversaries, birthdays)
- Multi-generational families
- Typically one decision-maker researching for the group

---

### Positioning Statement (Internal)

Oceana is the only Southeast Alaska private yacht charter where a classically trained chef and USCG-licensed captain guide a group of six through remote wilderness inaccessible to cruise ships — with every detail handled end-to-end.

---

### Differentiation (Core Advantages)

#### 1. The Chef (Ari Briens)
- Real story: nomadic, Mediterranean background
- Competitors: generic “gourmet chef” positioning
- Opportunity: make this a centerpiece, not a bullet

#### 2. High-Impact Testimonials
- Example:
  > “Streets ahead of anywhere we’ve travelled in Europe”
- Currently buried → should be front-loaded
- Strongest conversion asset

#### 3. Itinerary Specificity
- Named anchorages and route
- No competitor does this clearly
- Signals expertise + makes experience tangible

---

### Strategic Constraint

**Do NOT compete on price**

- Price sensitivity = wrong customer
- Signal premium → attract premium buyer

---

## 2. Offer Structure

### Core Offer

**Southeast Alaska Private Expedition**  
- 8 Days / 7 Nights  
- Juneau → ABC Islands → Return  
- Up to 6 guests (private vessel)  
- Full crew:
  - Captain Michael  
  - Chef Ari  
  - First Mate Dom  
  - Stewardess Lysanne  

> Avoid the word “package” → use **expedition**

---

### What’s Included (Frame as Abundance)

- All meals (chef-prepared onboard)
- All fuel, navigation, anchorage
- Kayaking, fishing, wildlife viewing
- Glacier access
- Crew of 4 for 6 guests
- House alcohol included
- No hidden costs

---

### Pricing Strategy

**Framing first, price second**

> “Private. All-inclusive. Eight days your group will talk about for decades.”

Then:

> **From $X for up to 6 guests**

---

### Value Reframe

- ~$70,000 total
- ~$1,666/person/night

Position as:
- Comparable to luxury hotel suite
- But includes:
  - private vessel
  - chef
  - crew
  - full experience

---

### Availability (Scarcity)


2026 Season — Limited Availability

Aug 8–15 — BOOKED
June 14–21 — Available
[Remaining dates] — Available


- Real scarcity > fake urgency

## 5. Page Structure (Top → Bottom)

---

### 1. Hero

**Goal:** Stop scroll + trigger action

- Full-bleed image
- Headline:
  > “Your Own Private Alaska — No Crowds, No Compromises”
- Subheadline:
  > Private yacht charter through Southeast Alaska wilderness
- CTA: Check Availability

---

### 2. Experience Proof

**Goal:** Make it feel real

- 3–4 images:
  - deck life
  - food
  - wildlife
  - scenery
- Callouts:
  - Remote locations
  - Private chef
  - Wildlife access
- Include 1 testimonial

---

### 3. Offer

**Goal:** Clarity

- Charter overview
- What’s included
- Price framing
- CTA

---

### 4. Itinerary

**Goal:** Build desire via specificity

Route example:

Juneau → White Stone Harbor → Tenakee Springs → Basket Bay → Cosmo Cove → Red Bluff Bay → The Brothers → Glacier → Holkham Bay → Juneau


- Add context about SE Alaska uniqueness
- CTA: View full itinerary

---

### 5. Crew

**Goal:** Human trust

- 4 crew cards
- Names + credentials + short bios

---

### 6. Availability

**Goal:** Urgency

- Calendar-style display
- Highlight booked vs available

---

### 7. Testimonials

**Goal:** Peer validation

- 3–4 detailed testimonials
- Emotional + experiential mix

### 8. Inquiry Form

- Name
