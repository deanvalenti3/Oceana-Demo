# Research Folder Index

## Purpose
Stores competitor analysis and market research for the Oceana website overhaul.

## Files
- `client-site-teardown.md` — weaknesses and missed conversion opportunities on the current site
- `core-competitors.md` — deep analysis of client-provided competitors
- `local-competitors.md` — additional Alaska competitors
- `national-benchmarks.md` — strong national comparison sites
- `funnel-insights.md` — patterns and conclusions from all research
- `homepage-strategy.md` — final strategy derived from research

## Rule
Do not build from raw research alone. Use research to update planning docs first.
